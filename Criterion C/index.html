Conflict Resolution and Management for Students

What is Conflict Resolution and Management


Conflict can be defiend as a disagreement between 2 or more parties. They can be caused by multiple things, but the best way to deal with them is through effective negotiation and conflict management. Conflict resolution can simply be defined as the means by which 2 or more parties solve a problem or conflict they are facing. The aim of conflict resolution is to solve a disagreement peacefully and in a way that benefits all parties involved.

Why is it important

Being able to resolve conflicts is a really important skill, especially in group tasks. There are many instances in group tasks where the team members fight due to disagreements over plans and decisions, unequal participation from team members, etc. The issue with these fights is that they waste a lot of valuable time and reduce team morale and productivity. Having conflict resolution skills, and knowing how to deal with these problems is incredibly helpful, because they allow the team members to solve and avoid conflicts, while maintaining productivity and working together efficiently.

Developing your conflict resolution skills improves your communication skills and your social skills. Having these skills are also a big part of being a good leader, and they aren't only valuable in school settings, many companies look for good conflict resolution skills especially while recruiting managers.
